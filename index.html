Concept

"Tree plantation drives are one of the major events for the earth, it helps a lot to retain greenery on earth. But it’s not the reality we know all of these plantation drives are not that successful as according to surveys and a report from TIMES OF INDIA “Survival rate of plants after last year’s green drives is 50-60%” only that means we had killed a lot of trees till now. so, we come up with a novel idea "mittvaanee" means The voice of soil is basically what our device will do it will collect important information about soil like moisture, elements in the soil, and information about the environment with the help sensors then a machine learning model which process the data and gives a list of trees or plants that need to be planted at that spot so that it can grow easily and fast as per the soil voice And it also Gio-tag (location tagging) that location to the person who had planted that tree and makes him notifies the condition of the tree as per the data collected Helps in reducing the loss of tree life as well as increases vegetation It had a lot of features as well has a web app for it also to enhance its user experience And it can also be helpful for farmers in their farming and keeping the track of records of soil at different points in India."
